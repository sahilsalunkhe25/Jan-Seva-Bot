Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY)

Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY) is a flagship scheme launched by the Government of India in 2015 to provide financial support to micro and small enterprises (MSEs) in the form of loans for their business activities. The scheme aims to promote entrepreneurship, generate employment opportunities, and foster economic growth by providing access to credit for the unserved and underserved segments of the population in India.

Objective: The main objective of PMMY is to provide financial support in the form of loans to micro and small enterprises (MSEs) for their business activities, which includes manufacturing, trading, and services sectors, and promote entrepreneurship among the economically weaker sections of society.
