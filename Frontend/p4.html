Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)

Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY) is a social security scheme launched by the Government of India in 2015. It is an accidental insurance scheme that aims to provide affordable accidental death and disability coverage to individuals across India, particularly those from economically weaker sections of society.

Objective: The main objective of PMSBY is to provide financial security and insurance coverage against accidental death and disability to individuals at an affordable premium, especially to those who are economically vulnerable and do not have access to insurance coverage.
