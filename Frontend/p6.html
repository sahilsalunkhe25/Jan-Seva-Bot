Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-Kisan)

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-Kisan) is a government of India scheme launched in 2018 to provide financial assistance to small and marginal farmers across the country. The scheme aims to provide direct income support to farmers to meet their agricultural expenses and improve their economic well-being.

Objective: The main objective of PM-Kisan is to provide financial assistance to small and marginal farmers in the form of direct income support. The scheme aims to ensure a stable income for farmers, enable them to meet their agricultural expenses, and support their livelihoods.
