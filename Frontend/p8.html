Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan-Dhan Yojana (PM-KMY)

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan-Dhan Yojana (PM-KMY) is a government of India initiative aimed at providing social security to small and marginal farmers in the form of a pension after the age of 60. It was launched in September 2019 and is a voluntary and contributory pension scheme for farmers.

Objective: The main objective of PM-KMY is to provide financial protection and social security to small and marginal farmers in their old age. The scheme aims to ensure that farmers have a regular pension income after the age of 60, enabling them to meet their living expenses and have a dignified retirement.
