Jan Dhan Yojana

Jan Dhan Yojana, officially known as Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY), is a financial inclusion scheme launched by the Government of India in 2014. The scheme aims to provide affordable financial services, including bank accounts, insurance, credit, and pension, to all households in India, especially the economically marginalized sections of society.

Objective: The main objective of PMJDY is to ensure comprehensive financial inclusion by providing access to basic banking services to all households in the country, with a special focus on the unbanked and underbanked population.
